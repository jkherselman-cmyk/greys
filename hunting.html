Hunting Overview

A Premier Eastern Cape Hunting Destination

The Eastern Cape is widely regarded as one of Africa's finest hunting destinations — a land of sweeping valleys, dramatic ridgelines, and an extraordinary diversity of plains game. Grey's Gift Lodge puts you at the heart of it all.

Our professional hunters have decades of experience guiding both seasoned hunters and those on their first African safari. Every hunt is a bespoke experience — tailored to your species preferences, schedule, and hunting style. Whether you prefer a challenging stalk on foot or a patient wait at a carefully chosen hide, we deliver unforgettable memories.

Our Approach

Ethical Hunting & Conservation

At Grey's Gift Lodge, we believe that responsible, ethical hunting is one of the most powerful tools for conservation. Every hunt is conducted within strict quotas and in full compliance with South African wildlife regulations.

We are proud members of PHASA (Professional Hunters' Association of South Africa) and operate under the principles of fair chase, sustainable yield, and zero-waste utilisation of harvested animals. Trophy fees directly fund wildlife management, habitat restoration, and community upliftment.

  • PHASA-registered professional hunters
  • All hunts conducted under CNC permit
  • Fair chase principles observed at all times
  • Revenue supports conservation & community
