The Lodge

Where Rest Meets Wilderness

Grey's Gift Lodge accommodates up to 16 guests in comfortable, en-suite rooms designed with nature in mind. Each room opens onto the sweeping views that make the Eastern Cape so unforgettable — mountain ridgelines at sunrise, the glint of waterholes at dusk.

Rooms & Suites

Our Accommodation

Standard Room

Deluxe Twin Room

Spacious twin-bedded rooms with en-suite bathroom, private veranda overlooking the natural landscape, air conditioning, and full-length windows to maximise the views.

  • Twin or king configuration available
  • En-suite shower and bathroom
  • Private veranda
  • Air conditioning & ceiling fan
Superior Room

Superior Double Suite

Our superior rooms feature a king-size bed, a separate lounge area, a larger en-suite with both bath and shower, and an extended veranda with panoramic mountain views.

  • King-size bed
  • Lounge area with fireplace
  • Bath and walk-in shower
  • Extended veranda & outdoor seating
Family Room

Family Cottage

Perfect for families, our cottage features two interconnecting rooms, a shared lounge, and a private outdoor area with braai facilities — ideal for longer stays.

  • Two interconnecting bedrooms (up to 5 guests)
  • Private lounge & kitchen kitchenette
  • Private braai area
  • Children's bunk bed available on request
