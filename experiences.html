Nature & Wildlife

The Eastern Cape in All Its Splendour

The farm Grey's Gift sits within one of South Africa's most biodiverse regions. Five of the country's nine biomes are represented in the Eastern Cape, and the farm's varied terrain — from rocky kloofs to open bushveld — supports a remarkable abundance of flora and fauna.

  • 30+ species of naturally occurring plains game
  • Over 200 bird species recorded on the farm
  • Guided nature walks with expert trackers
  • Indigenous vegetation walks with a botanist (on request)
  • Natural waterholes for passive wildlife viewing
Family Activities

Adventures for All Ages

Grey's Gift Lodge is the perfect family destination — a place where children can discover nature, parents can unwind, and grandparents can share in the magic of the African bush. Our team creates tailored activities to ensure every member of the family has an unforgettable experience.

  • Guided game drives suitable for all ages
  • Children's farm activities and animal encounters
  • Outdoor braai evenings and stargazing
  • Waterhole hide visits at dawn and dusk
  • Guided walks and gentle nature trails
Photography

Safari Photography

The Eastern Cape offers some of Africa's most dramatic light — from golden sunrise over the Winterberg Mountains to the fiery hues of an Eastern Cape sunset. Grey's Gift is a paradise for the nature photographer.

Golden Hours

Sunrise & Sunset Drives

Join our guides for dedicated photography drives timed around the golden hour, when wildlife is most active and light most magical.

Hides

Fixed Waterhole Hides

Position yourself at one of our carefully situated hides overlooking natural waterholes. Animals approach to within metres — perfect for close-up photography.

Astrophotography

Night Sky Photography

Far from light pollution, the skies above Grey's Gift deliver a breathtaking Milky Way. Bring your wide-angle lens and tripod for an unforgettable night shoot.

Waterhole Watching

Nature's Theatre at Dawn & Dusk

The farm's multiple natural waterholes are the heartbeat of wildlife activity on Grey's Gift. From your room's veranda or from the lodge's viewing deck, you can watch kudu, springbok, warthog, and a parade of birdlife arrive to drink — morning coffee has never been this spectacular.

Our dedicated hides are positioned within prime wildlife traffic zones and can be booked exclusively by one party at a time, for a truly private nature experience.
