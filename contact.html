Getting Here

Arriving at Grey's Gift

By Air

Port Elizabeth / Gqeberha

Fly into OR Tambo Gqeberha Airport (PLZ). Grey's Gift Lodge is approximately 2 hours by road. Airport transfer can be arranged upon request.

By Road

Self-Drive from Makhanda

Approximately 40 minutes from Makhanda (Grahamstown) via the R67. Detailed GPS co-ordinates and route notes will be sent with your booking confirmation.

Transfers

Arranged Transfers

Lodge-to-airport transfer services available from PLZ, East London or King William's Town. Contact us when booking to arrange door-to-door collection.
