Welcome to Grey's Gift Lodge

An Unforgettable African Experience

Grey's Gift Lodge offers a tailor-made hunting safari and family holiday with accommodation in a modern, luxury lodge with a homely atmosphere. Nestled in a quiet, rural corner of the Eastern Cape, we cater for hunters, observers, and families who want to relax and rediscover nature at its most beautiful.

Situated on the family farm Grey's Gift — farmed across five generations — just two hours from Gqeberha (Port Elizabeth) Airport and forty minutes from Makhanda (Grahamstown), in the heart of one of South Africa's finest hunting destinations.

Malaria-Free

Enjoy the wild Eastern Cape without health concerns — perfectly safe year-round for all ages.

Family Heritage

Five generations of this family farm, rich with tradition and authentic African warmth.

Luxury Accommodation

Modern, comfortable lodging with breathtaking views of the mountains and valleys.

Ethical Hunting

Sustainable hunting safaris curated for both the seasoned hunter and intrepid first-timer.

Accommodation

Stay in Timeless Comfort

Our modern luxury lodge blends contemporary comfort with the raw beauty of the Eastern Cape. Unwind on spacious verandas overlooking mountain valleys and natural waterholes, where wildlife roams freely at dawn and dusk.

  • En-suite rooms with stunning mountain views
  • Communal lounge and outdoor braai area
  • Full-board dining with traditional South African cuisine
  • Wi-Fi, air conditioning, and modern amenities

Hunting Safaris

The Ultimate Eastern Cape Safari

The Eastern Cape is one of Africa's premier hunting destinations, home to a rich variety of indigenous game. Our tailor-made packages cater to hunters of all experience levels, guided by seasoned professional hunters who know every kopje and kloof of the farm.

  • 30+ species of plains game available
  • Professional hunters and experienced guides
  • Sustainable, ethical hunting practices
  • Custom packages for individuals and groups

Our Mission

Conservation & Community

Our mission is to offer an unforgettable African experience while preserving the natural heritage of the Eastern Cape. We are deeply committed to sustainable land management, ethical hunting, and investing in the livelihoods of those in our local community.

Grey's Gift Lodge participates in conservation initiatives that protect the rich biodiversity of this remarkable landscape — ensuring it thrives for the next five generations and beyond.
