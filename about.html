The Lodge

A Modern Luxury Lodge with a Homely Soul

Built on the verge of a hill with nature on your doorstep, Grey's Gift Lodge commands a magnificent view of the surrounding mountains, open valleys, and natural waterholes. It's the kind of place where you arrive as a guest and leave as family.

The lodge combines the luxury of modern facilities with an authentic, welcoming atmosphere. From the generous verandas where the African sunset puts on its nightly show, to the fireplace that keeps evenings warm — every detail has been considered for your comfort.

  • Luxury en-suite accommodation for up to 16 guests
  • Panoramic views across the Eastern Cape mountains
  • Multiple natural waterholes visible from the lodge
  • Outdoor braai boma and communal lapa
  • Full-board catering with farm-to-table dining
Our Story

The Farm Grey's Gift — A Century of Belonging

The farm Grey's Gift has been in the family for five generations. What began as a working farm more than a century ago has evolved into one of the Eastern Cape's finest private lodges, while retaining the heritage, warmth, and connection to the land that defines its character.

Each generation has built upon the last — developing the land with care, introducing wildlife, protecting indigenous flora, and welcoming guests from around the world. It is a living legacy, shaped by love for this extraordinary corner of Africa.

Getting Here

Location & Directions

Grey's Gift Lodge is ideally situated in the heart of the malaria-free Eastern Cape — easily accessible from major centres yet beautifully remote.

By Air

Fly into Gqeberha (Port Elizabeth) Airport, then drive approximately 2 hours on the N2 and R67 routes. Car rental is available at the airport.

By Road

Located 40 minutes from Makhanda (Grahamstown) and 2 hours from Gqeberha. GPS coordinates and detailed directions provided on booking.

Transfer Service

We can arrange comfortable transfers from Gqeberha Airport or Makhanda directly to the lodge. Please enquire when booking.

Our Mission

Preserving the Eastern Cape for Future Generations

Our mission is to offer an unforgettable African experience, to preserve nature, ensure sustainable hunting, and provide meaningful employment for people from our local community.

We believe in the harmony between responsible hunting and conservation. Sustainable hunting has long been an integral part of land stewardship in the Eastern Cape, helping to fund wildlife management and habitat protection. Grey's Gift Lodge is committed to practices that ensure the ecological health of this farm for generations to come.

  • Active wildlife and habitat management
  • Ethical hunting guided by PHASA standards
  • Employment and skills development for local communities
  • Indigenous vegetation restoration and protection
